About Me关于我

I'm Rick — a film & media producer turned hardware hacker and AI toolmaker.


My journey started on film sets, where I learned to manage chaos, coordinate teams, and bring creative visions to life under tight deadlines. That hands-on production experience shaped how I think about building systems.


Now I split my time between managing productions, studying management at CIAM, and hacking on hardware/software projects. I wire up ESP32 boards, build AI comic pipelines with ComfyUI and FLUX, and automate everything I can touch.


I believe the most interesting work happens when you refuse to pick a lane — when film production thinking meets embedded systems meets generative AI.

我是 Rick——一个从影视制片人转型的硬件极客和 AI 工具开发者。


我的旅程始于片场。在那里我学会了管理混乱、协调团队、在紧迫的截止日期下将创意变为现实。这些实战经验塑造了我对系统构建的思维方式。


现在我在制片管理、CIAM 管理学学业，以及硬件/软件项目之间切换。我焊接 ESP32 开发板、用 ComfyUI 和 FLUX 搭建 AI 漫画流水线、自动化一切可以自动化的东西。


我相信最有趣的工作发生在你拒绝只走一条路的时候——当影视制片思维遇上嵌入式系统遇上生成式 AI。
